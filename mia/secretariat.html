Secretariat of The Interior Affairs & Public Security

The Ministerial Board is chaired by the Secretary of State for the Interior Affairs, non-executive directors, and secretary staff.
It forms the collective strategic leadership of the Interior Affairs Authority with a broad purpose to steer and scrutinise the department's strategy,performance and capability. Its remit is wide-ranging with the top-level committeees and timebound boards reporting directly into it.
